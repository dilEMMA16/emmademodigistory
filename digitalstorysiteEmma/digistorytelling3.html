Nestled among the memorable memes, purrrrr-fection of cat videos, and Netflix documentaries about tiny houses on the World Wide Web, a quaint website by the name inspiHER was hosted. This website was home to a young and curious HTML tag with no true name but who went simply by < meta >.


< meta > had been nameless for as long as she could remember and perpetually felt a bit like she was missing something because of it. However, < meta > adored life on inspiHER because she got to inspire women to acquaint themselves with all the possibilities of STEM, but sometimes < meta > wished she would be able to reach more people.


One day, the Dev Queen Seo, ruler of all the World Wide Web, saw a bit of herself in < meta > and wanted to use her collection of resources to empower less experienced novice < meta > because she recognized the importance for beginners to have strong, relatable role models in their tech treks.


Determined to be this positive figure in < meta >’s tango with technology, Seo grabbed her adventure accomplice and fluffy pet, F12, and with a click of a link, the duo transported to inspiHER to mentor < meta >.


Upon their arrival, F12 found < meta > and ran up to greet her. “OMGeek, it’s YOU!” squealed < meta >, overflowing with excitement when she saw Seo, “What are you guys doing here of all places?”

“I have a gift for you that I hope can help you be successful. Have you ever heard of Search Engine Optimization by chance?” inquired Seo. < meta > shook her head no.


“Well now we have an opportunity to learn and grow then,” smiled Seo. “Search Engine Optimization is a way to help increase the visibility of a website by making it easier to discover with tools like Google! With it, I believe you will be able to get more visitors on inspiHER, therefore increasing your positive impact.”

“That sounds SPECTACULAR,” exclaimed < meta >, giddy with the idea of helping more women be inspired by STEM, “How do I use it?!”


Based on my expertise and research, I’ve identified that you seem to be missing a name,” explained Seo. “So today, to help you utilize Search Engine Optimization, I am going to honor you with three: Description, Viewport, and Robots.” Instantly, < meta > experienced wholesomeness in a completely new way.

“Description will give you the power to summarize your website’s content, Viewport will allow your website to resize when needed to enhance your users’ experiences, and Robots will tell search engines what to look at,” continued Seo.


“Thank you from my < head > to my < toes >!” beamed < meta >, “I will use them strategically and never forget your kindness and mentorship toward me.”

Seo smiled, “I wish you the best in your tech trek, and remember these three snippets of advice that have helped me continue to grow: 1) Ask questions, they help you learn! 2) Always exercise your curiosity by being a life-long learner and 3) Practice in hands-on ways. With that, I must get on my way again.” With a click of the back button, Seo and F12 vanished once again.


The next day,< meta > woke up to a crowd of eager visitors on inspiHER, all waiting enthusiastically to hear her message about the limitless possibilities and opportunities in STEM for women of all backgrounds and skill levels.

And so the next era of < meta >’s movement of empowerment began.
